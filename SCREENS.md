# Screens — Freelancer-portal

Per-screen documentation (purpose · flow · validations) for the freelancer-facing product.

## login.html
**Purpose:** Freelancer logs in to access their contracts, payments and team.
**Flow:** Entry point → on success, lands on the freelancer home/onboarding.

## freelancer-onboarding.html
**Purpose:** A freelancer onboards themselves into Wisemonk (profile, payout, compliance details).
**Flow:** Reached after login / invite. On completion the freelancer reaches their dashboard.
